Portfolio closed April $4.43M on rooms · +5.7% vs budget.

Standout closes: ROOST Tampa at $843,736 (+17% vs budget), The Pinch Charleston at $497,185 on a 92% Apr final occ, and ROOST East Market at $302,961 (+20% vs budget). ROOST Cleveland remains a structural concern — RevPAR Index at 85.8 against the comp set, even though April closed within 2 percent of budget. May open is healthy — portfolio May OTB is $3,242,500 with Method Co's wider rollup forecasting +197 rooms pickup overnight.
